# Experiments

## Purpose

Future home of reproducible investigations that are not production dependencies.

## Future Responsibilities

Hypotheses, controlled comparisons, reproducible configurations, results, limitations, and explicit graduation or rejection decisions.

## Ownership

Conducting researcher or engineer — `<OWNER_NAME>`.

## What Belongs Here

Self-contained experimental code, lightweight configurations, result summaries, and links to governed artifacts.

## What Should Not Belong Here

Production runtime imports, irreplaceable logic, secrets, unlicensed data, unexplained notebooks, or large generated artifacts.

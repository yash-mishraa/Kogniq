# Datasets

## Purpose

Future governance point for datasets used by research, evaluation, and production pipelines.

## Future Responsibilities

Dataset manifests, provenance, licenses, schemas, versions, quality reports, access rules, retention, and reproducible acquisition instructions.

## Ownership

Data engineering and governance team — `<OWNER_NAME>`.

## What Belongs Here

Small approved metadata, manifests, schemas, data cards, and acquisition or validation documentation.

## What Should Not Belong Here

Secrets, personal data without governance, copyrighted material without rights, untracked dumps, large binaries, or model code.

# Knowledge Graph Package

## Purpose

Reserve Kogniq's domain-neutral ontology, curriculum-graph, provenance, and graph-query boundary.

## Responsibilities

Future node and edge semantics, graph validation, curriculum mappings, versioning, provenance, ingestion contracts, and traversal behavior.

## Public Interface

Planned node/edge schemas, validation reports, graph queries, traversals, and import/export contracts. Nothing is implemented.

## Dependencies

May depend on `packages/shared`, approved domain-plugin contracts, and future persistence adapters behind owned ports.

## Future Expansion

Temporal validity, assisted curation, multilingual labels, cross-domain mappings, and alternate graph backends after requirements are measured.

## Ownership

Knowledge engineering team with domain reviewers — `<OWNER_NAME>`.

## What Does NOT Belong Here

Generic persistence utilities, UI, product workflow orchestration, undocumented scraped content, learner-private data, retrieval pipelines, or assumptions tied to one examination.
